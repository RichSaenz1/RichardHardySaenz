Common questions

QUESTIONS
ABOUT THE
ACADEMY

What do I receive inside the Academy?+

You receive live instruction, practical examples, selected templates, workflow guidance, and a clearer way to apply AI to real creative or business work. The exact depth depends on whether you choose a workshop, studio pass, or full program.

Is the Academy for beginners or advanced teams?+

Both. Beginners get a clear path into practical AI use, while more advanced users can focus on workflows, branding, content systems, automation thinking, and implementation quality.

Do I need technical experience?+

No. The Academy is designed around applied use, creative direction, business workflows, and practical decision-making rather than coding-first complexity.

Are sessions live or self-paced?+

The Academy focuses on live formats first: single workshop, studio pass, and full studio program. Recordings and materials depend on the chosen format.

Can I join in English or Spanish?+

Yes. The Academy is structured for English, Spanish, and bilingual delivery where needed.

Can a company book team training?+

Yes. Teams can use the Academy as a learning layer before, during, or after implementation so the people inside the business understand how to use AI properly.

Which format should I choose first?+

Choose a Single Workshop if you want one focused result, Studio Pass if you want repeated guided sessions, and Full Studio Program if you want a deeper transformation across multiple workflows.
