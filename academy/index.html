Course tracks

What you actually build

Each Academy track is built around practical studio outcomes. You learn the tools, but the goal is always a finished brand, portfolio, story, campaign, or business asset you can use.

Creative

Design, Branding & AI

Create a complete brand identity from initial concept to final presentation, blending design thinking with AI research, visualisation, storytelling, and brand systems.

  • Define brand purpose, audience, message, and emotional direction.
  • Research competitors, moodboards, visual strategy, and brand personality.
  • Develop logo concepts, colour, typography, composition, and interface ideas.
  • Create a one-page brand summary, expansion concept, and final presentation.
Business

AI for Creatives & Business

Build and launch a brand or creative business using AI for strategy, identity, websites, content, marketing automation, storytelling, and launch planning.

  • Turn an idea into a clear brand purpose, audience, and business direction.
  • Build strategy, visual identity, brand story, messaging, and website structure.
  • Create branded content, campaign assets, voice/video, and automated workflows.
  • Prepare a launch-ready creative business foundation and final presentation.
Portfolio

Portfolio & Personal Branding

Design, write, and build a professional creative identity using AI to refine your story, portfolio structure, presentation, and online presence.

  • Discover your creative identity, artist statement, bio, tagline, and visual language.
  • Structure portfolio projects, write project stories, and create polished mockups.
  • Build a digital portfolio or presentation with cohesive branding and case studies.
  • Develop visibility, personal brand consistency, and a final portfolio review.
Advanced

Visual Storytelling & Branding

Use AI-powered motion, storyboarding, animation, voice, and sound to create a complete short-form brand story for a person, brand, or business.

  • Define message, audience, emotional tone, mood, and visual direction.
  • Write a 6-scene script, storyboard frames, and plan pacing and transitions.
  • Use Runway, Pika, ElevenLabs, Photoshop, and Gamma to test motion and sound.
  • Complete a 30-60 second brand story or short-form narrative asset.

Tools you practice

AI with human direction

The Academy uses AI as a creative partner, not a replacement. You learn when to prompt, when to edit, when to design, and how to turn tool output into work that feels intentional.

Strategy & writing

ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Jasper for research, positioning, tone, taglines, bios, campaign copy, and story structure.

Visual design

Midjourney, Photoshop, Pinterest, Gamma, Uizard, and Galileo for moodboards, identity systems, layouts, interfaces, and presentations.

Motion & voice

Runway, Pika, Synthesia, and ElevenLabs for AI-assisted video, motion tests, narration, sound, and brand storytelling.

Systems & launch

Notion, Zapier, Codex, websites, social workflows, and automation thinking for organising assets and launching practical work.

Common questions

Frequently asked

What is Future Studio Academy?+

Future Studio Academy is a live learning program for AI, branding, portfolio building, business creativity, visual storytelling, and practical creative workflows.

Do I need previous AI experience?+

The basic build is for a focused first version. Studio Pass and Full Program are designed for multi-page websites with stronger service structure, assistant logic, and deeper conversion paths.

Are sessions in English and Spanish?+

Yes. Yes. The academy can support English, Spanish, or bilingual groups depending on the format and audience.

What format should I choose?+

Choose Single Workshop for one topic, Studio Pass for four focused sessions, and Full Studio Program if you want a guided journey with feedback and certificate.

Will I get recordings and templates?+

Yes. The website can route leads to Calendly, WhatsApp, email, intake forms, CRM tools, spreadsheets, or a dashboard depending on how your team works.

Is there a certificate?+

The Full Studio Program includes a certificate of completion. Single Workshop and Studio Pass are focused learning formats without full-program certification.
