# TFS Course Series Reference

## Funnel A — Plug the Leaks

- Series name: `Stop Losing Leads — A 5-Step AI Response System`
- Series promise: `By the end of these 5 videos, you'll have a working system that catches every missed lead, responds in under 60 seconds, books appointments automatically, and reports back to you every week.`

### Video 1 — Find Your Leaks
Run the revenue audit on your own business in 30 minutes and map where leads are disappearing across missed calls, unanswered WhatsApp, slow email replies, and after-hours gaps. By the end, you know which single leak to close first for the biggest return.

### Video 2 — Never Miss a Lead Again
Build a Make.com automation that detects every missed call or form submission and sends an instant personalized WhatsApp reply. By the end, your business responds to new leads in under 60 seconds automatically.

### Video 3 — Write an AI That Sounds Like You
Build the response-agent prompt with tone, boundaries, FAQ handling, and human handoff rules. By the end, you have a working AI assistant ready to deploy.

### Video 4 — Turn Messages Into Appointments
Connect Calendly or Google Calendar so leads move from first message into confirmed appointment automatically. By the end, your calendar fills itself.

### Video 5 — Know What's Working Every Monday
Build the weekly reporting automation that shows what was recovered, what still leaks, and what to fix next. By the end, you have a self-running Monday report.

## Funnel B — First Steps

- Series name: `From Zero to Your First AI System — A Practical 5-Step Guide`
- Series promise: `By the end of these 5 videos, you'll understand exactly what AI tools your business needs, you'll have built your first chatbot, written your business's AI brain, automated your follow-up, and have a clear 90-day plan for what to build next.`

### Video 1 — The AI Stack You Actually Need
Choose the messaging channel, automation hub, AI brain, and scheduler your business actually needs and ignore the rest. By the end, the stack decision is made.

### Video 2 — Build Your First Chatbot (No Code)
Deploy a working bot on your site or WhatsApp using your own FAQs. By the end, it is live.

### Video 3 — Write Your Business's AI Brain
Build the system prompt that defines personality, boundaries, knowledge, and handoff rules. By the end, you own a reusable working prompt.

### Video 4 — The Follow-Up That Books Appointments While You Sleep
Create the three-message automated follow-up with the exact structure, timing, and personalization logic needed to keep leads moving. By the end, your follow-up runs on its own.

### Video 5 — Your 90-Day AI Rollout Plan
Fill in the planning template used for new clients so the business knows what to build first, second, and third. By the end, you have a specific 90-day plan.
