What gets installed

A pipeline system with real logic

A lead pipeline should make every opportunity easier to see, qualify, assign, and follow up. This build connects the first lead source to the next action so your team knows what matters and what needs attention.

01

Lead capture

Collect leads from forms, WhatsApp, website assistants, email, calls, spreadsheets, or other entry points.

02

Qualification rules

Sort leads by service need, fit, urgency, source, value, location, or next best action.

03

Owner routing

Send the right lead to the right person, stage, inbox, calendar path, or CRM owner.

04

Follow-up tasks

Create reminders, next steps, handoff notes, and status updates instead of relying on memory.

05

Pipeline visibility

See where leads enter, which ones are warm, who owns them, and where they are getting stuck.

06

Clean handoff

Keep context attached so a lead can move from capture to call, quote, booking, or follow-up.

Why it matters

Warm leads should not vanish between tools

Most missed sales opportunities are not lost because the team does not care. They are lost because the source, owner, status, urgency, and next step are unclear. This system gives every lead a visible path from first contact to follow-up.

Common questions

Frequently asked

What does Lead Pipeline Automation do?+

It captures leads from your key sources, organises their details, qualifies them, routes them to the right owner or stage, and creates follow-up so opportunities do not disappear.

Can it work with our CRM?+

Yes. It can connect with a CRM, spreadsheet, email, forms, WhatsApp, website assistant, calendar, or task tool depending on your current setup and access.

Can it score or qualify leads?+

Yes. We can create simple rules around fit, urgency, service need, budget signal, location, source, or next best action.

Can this work in English and Spanish?+

Yes. Intake questions, notifications, summaries, and follow-up messages can be prepared for English, Spanish, or bilingual teams.

What monthly costs should I expect?+

The build price is separate from monthly CRM tools, automation tools, AI usage, email, WhatsApp, forms, dashboards, or other third-party services. We confirm expected monthly costs before build.

Do we need a complicated sales process first?+

No. We can start with a simple pipeline: new lead, qualified lead, follow-up needed, booked, won, lost, or whatever stages match your business.
