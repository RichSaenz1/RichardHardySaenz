Common questions

Frequently asked

What does the WhatsApp automation agent do?+

It replies to common WhatsApp questions, collects useful lead details, qualifies interest, sends the right next step, and routes conversations to your team when needed.

Can it replace manual WhatsApp replies?+

It can handle repeatable replies and intake, but it should not replace your team for sensitive, urgent, or high-value conversations. Those get handed off.

Can it work in English and Spanish?+

Yes. Replies, intake questions, reminders, and handoff messages can be prepared for English, Spanish, or bilingual audiences.

What monthly costs should I expect?+

The build price is separate from monthly WhatsApp provider costs, automation tools, AI usage, CRM, email, or other third-party services. We confirm the expected monthly costs before build.

Can it connect to Calendly, CRM, email, or forms?+

Yes. The WhatsApp flow can route leads to Calendly, forms, CRM tools, spreadsheets, email, dashboards, or a human handoff depending on how your team works.

Can it send reminders and follow-ups?+

Yes. The follow-up build can include reminders, confirmations, no-response nudges, booking links, and next-step messages.
